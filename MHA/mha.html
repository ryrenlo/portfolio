About - Welcome to MHA!

In a world where nearly everyone has a superpower known as a "Quirk," Izuku Midoriya is born Quirkless but dreams of becoming a hero. After a chance encounter with the world's greatest hero--All Might, Midoriya inherits his Quirk and enrolls in U.A. High School, a prestigious academy for aspiring heroes. The series follows Midoriya and his classmates as they train, hone their powers, and face threats from both villains and the pressures of hero society. Themes of courage, responsibility, and perseverance are central to the story.
